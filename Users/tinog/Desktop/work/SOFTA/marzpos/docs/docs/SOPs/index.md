# Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

Welcome to the MarzPOS Standard Operating Procedures documentation. These SOPs ensure consistent, efficient, and compliant operations across all aspects of Vape Garage.

Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) are detailed, written instructions to achieve uniformity in performing specific business functions. Following these procedures ensures:

- **Consistency** - Every staff member follows the same process
- **Quality** - Standards are maintained across all operations
- **Compliance** - Legal and regulatory requirements are met
- **Efficiency** - Tasks are completed in the most effective way
- **Training** - New staff have clear guidance

## üéØ How to Use These SOPs

### For New Staff
1. Start with **Staff Roles & Responsibilities** to understand your role
2. Review **Open and Close Procedures** for daily routines
3. Study **POS and Payment Processing** for transaction handling
4. Familiarize yourself with **Sales Procedures**

### For Existing Staff
- Refer to relevant SOPs when performing tasks
- Use as a reference when training new team members
- Report any outdated information to management

### For Managers
- Ensure all staff are trained on relevant SOPs
- Review SOPs regularly and update as needed
- Use SOPs during performance reviews and training sessions

## üîÑ SOP Lifecycle

### Review Schedule
- **Daily Operations SOPs**: Reviewed quarterly
- **Inventory SOPs**: Reviewed bi-annually
- **Staff SOPs**: Reviewed annually
- **All SOPs**: Updated immediately when processes change

### Version Control
Each SOP includes:
- Version number
- Creation/revision date
- Approval signature
- Next review date

### Suggesting Changes
If you identify improvements to an SOP:
1. Document the suggested change
2. Discuss with your manager
3. Submit formal change request
4. Manager reviews and approves/rejects
5. If approved, SOP is updated and re-distributed
