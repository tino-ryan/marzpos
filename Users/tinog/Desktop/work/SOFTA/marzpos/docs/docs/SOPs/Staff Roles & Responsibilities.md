### 1. Purpose

To clearly define duties, responsibilities, and authority levels for each role in the vape shop. This ensures smooth operations, accountability, efficient task delegation, and full compliance with company policies and legal requirements for age-restricted products.

### 2. Scope

**Who:** All staff (Cashiers, Store Manager, Store Owner)  
**When:** At all times during employment and operations  
**Where:** Entire store premises and related administrative tasks

### 3. Responsibility Overview

| Role            | Key Responsibilities Summary                                      |
|-----------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Cashier         | Daily operations, customer service, transactions, opening/closing, cleaning |
| Store Manager   | All Cashier duties + stock/supplier management, staff oversight, issue reporting |
| Store Owner     | Strategic oversight, major approvals, HR, payments, critical issue resolution |

### 4. Key Definitions

- **Cash-Up:** Daily reconciliation of till cash against POS sales records
- **Customer Management:** Handling enquiries, complaints, age verification, and sales

### 5. Required Tools & Systems

- Ultimate POS system (all roles)
- Company phone and communication tools (Manager/Owner)
- Cleaning supplies (Cashiers)
- Attendance tracking/clock-in (Manager)

### 6. Role Details

#### <a name="cashier-sop-role-001"></a>Cashier Responsibilities (SOP-ROLE-001)

**Core Duties:**
- Follow store opening and closing procedures (SOP-OPEN-001 & SOP-CLOSE-001)
- Perform accurate cash-up at end of shift/day
- Maintain cleanliness: counter, floor, displays, bins
- Provide excellent customer service: greet customers, answer questions, handle complaints
- Strictly perform age verification (ID check for anyone appearing under 30)
- Process all transactions accurately in Ultimate POS (sales, returns, authorised discounts)

**Critical:**  
No sale without valid ID — refusal of sale if ID not provided. Zero exceptions.

#### <a name="store-manager-sop-role-002"></a>Store Manager Responsibilities (SOP-ROLE-002)

**Core Duties (includes all Cashier duties when required):**
- Monitor stock levels, place orders, receive stock, perform adjustments and stock takes
- Interact with suppliers (orders, queries, discrepancies)
- Track staff attendance, punctuality, and performance
- Train and supervise Cashiers
- Report operational issues, discrepancies, or maintenance needs to Owner promptly

**Critical:**  
Escalate any issue impacting sales or security immediately. Do not approve bulk/large orders without Owner sign-off.

#### <a name="store-owner-sop-role-003"></a>Store Owner Responsibilities (SOP-ROLE-003)

**Core Duties:**
- Resolve critical issues (e.g., POS/card machine failure, alarm faults)
- Approve bulk or high-value stock orders
- Assign tasks and shifts
- Manage HR: hiring, discipline, terminations, performance reviews
- Handle payroll, supplier payments, financial oversight
- Review reports and ensure SOP/legal compliance

**Critical:**  
All major financial and disciplinary actions must be documented.

#### <a name="ultimate-pos-user-management-sop-role-004"></a>Ultimate POS User Management (SOP-ROLE-004)

**Core Duties (Owner or designated Superadmin only):**
- Create, edit, and disable user accounts to match staff roles
- Assign appropriate permissions:
  - Cashiers: limited (sales, POS only)
  - Manager: broader (stock, reports)
  - Owner: full access
- Add new staff users immediately upon hiring
- Disable/delete accounts immediately upon termination
- Regularly review user list for inactive accounts

**In Ultimate POS:**
- User Management → Add/List Users
- Assign roles with specific permissions
- Assign to correct business location
- Enable commission tracking if needed

**Critical:**  
Never share credentials. Use unique logins. Apply least-privilege access (e.g., Cashiers must not access stock adjustments or reports).

### 7. Daily/Weekly Verification Checklist

- [ ] Cashiers completing transactions and cleaning tasks

- [ ] POS user accounts and permissions up-to-date

### 8. Exceptions & Troubleshooting

| Issue                                  | Action Required                                                                 | Escalate To     |
|----------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------|
| Staff unclear on duties                | 1. Refer to this SOP<br />2. Ask Manager for clarification                     | Store Manager   |
| Role overlap/confusion                 | 1. Manager clarifies task ownership<br />2. Document for training              | Store Owner     |
| Issue outside role authority (e.g., POS down) | 1. Report immediately<br />2. Owner resolves                                   | Store Owner     |
| Forgotten POS password                 | 1. Superadmin resets via User Management                                       | Store Owner     |
| Suspected unauthorised access          | 1. Disable user immediately<br />2. Review activity logs                       | Store Owner     |

### 9. Compliance & Rules

**Legal Requirements**
- Strict age verification (minimum age 21 or per local law)
- Accurate transaction records for tax/audit

**Company Policies**
- Clear separation of duties
- No tasks outside authorised role without approval
- Individual POS accounts only

**Prohibited Actions**
- Cashiers ordering stock or approving large expenses
- Manager handling HR decisions without Owner
- Bypassing age verification
- Sharing POS login credentials

**Consequences**
- Immediate discipline (up to termination) for age-verification breaches
- Personal liability for unreported discrepancies
- Termination for repeated non-compliance or security breaches

### 10. Records & Reports

**Records to Maintain**
- Attendance Log — Daily — Manager
- Incident/Issue Reports — As needed — Shared folder
- POS User List — On changes — Ultimate POS

**Reports**
- Staff Performance Summary — Monthly — Store Owner
- Escalated Issues Log — As needed — Store Owner

**Retention**
- HR/Attendance: 7 years
- Issue Reports: Minimum 3 years
