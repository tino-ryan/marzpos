SOP TITLE: Discount Authorization & Application
SOP CODE: SOP-SALES-009
VERSION: 1.1
Updated: January 13, 2026 (verified UltimatePOS discount config & tax behavior)

  1. PURPOSE
    To ensure discounts are applied consistently, only when authorized/pre-configured, and in a way that maintains correct tax/VAT calculation. Protects revenue while allowing promotional flexibility.

  2. SCOPE

WHO:
‚Ä¢ Cashier (for auto-applied or approved discounts)
‚Ä¢ Shop Manager/Owner (for configuration & manual approvals)

WHEN:
‚Ä¢ During sales where discount eligibility exists (promotion, regular customer, etc.)

WHERE:
‚Ä¢ Sales counter / POS

  1. RESPONSIBILITY
ROLERESPONSIBILITY
CASHIERApply pre-configured discounts only; escalate manual requests
SHOP MANAGER/OWNERConfigure discounts; approve one-off/manual discounts
  1. DEFINITIONS

‚Ä¢ Pre-Configured Discount: Auto-applied via Sell > Discounts (brand/category/location-based)
‚Ä¢ Ad-Hoc/Manual Discount: Applied directly on POS screen during transaction
‚Ä¢ Authorization: Pre-setup by Manager or explicit approval for manual cases

  1. REQUIRED TOOLS / SYSTEMS
    ‚Ä¢ UltimatePOS system
    ‚Ä¢ Sell > Discounts menu (for config)
    ‚Ä¢ Manager approval for manual cases

  2. PROCEDURE (Step-by-Step)

STEP 1: Identify Discount Eligibility
ACTION: Check if sale qualifies for discount.
DETAIL:
‚Ä¢ Look for pre-configured promos (auto-apply on matching brand/category/location).
‚Ä¢ If customer requests manual discount ‚Üí escalate to Manager.
SYSTEM: POS screen (discount shows automatically if configured)
‚ö† CRITICAL: Do NOT apply manual discounts on POS screen unless Manager-approved and tested.

STEP 2: Prefer Pre-Configured Discounts
ACTION: Rely on system auto-application.
DETAIL:
‚Ä¢ Pre-configured discounts (set by Manager) apply automatically and correctly recalculate tax/VAT (discount before tax).
‚Ä¢ No cashier action needed ‚Äî verify discount appears on POS total.
SYSTEM: N/A (auto)
‚ö† CRITICAL: Ad-hoc discounts added directly on POS cash register screen during live transaction often cause tax miscalculation (tax on original amount, not discounted). Avoid this method.

STEP 3: Configure New/Targeted Discounts (Manager/Owner Only)
ACTION: Add discount via Sell menu.
DETAIL:

  1. Go to Sell > Discounts ‚Üí Click Add (top right).
  2. Fill fields:
    • Name: e.g., "Bolt 10% Jan Promo"
    • Brand: e.g., Bolt, Geekvape
    • Category: e.g., Disposable, Juices
    • Location: Vape Garage (required)
    • Priority: Higher number wins on overlaps (e.g., 10 > 5)
    • Discount Type: Fixed or Percentage
    • Discount Amount: e.g., 10 (for %)
    • Starts At / Ends At: Date range
    • Selling Price Group: All/Default or specific
    • Apply in customer groups: Optional
    • Is active: ON
  3. Save ‚Üí Test sale to confirm auto-apply & correct tax.
    SYSTEM: Sell > Discounts

STEP 4: Handle Manual/One-Off Requests
ACTION: Escalate to Manager.
DETAIL:
‚Ä¢ Inform customer discount requires approval.
‚Ä¢ Manager decides (approve/refuse) ‚Üí If approved, Manager applies or instructs cashier (test tax after).
SYSTEM: POS (manual entry if approved)
‚ö† CRITICAL: Manual POS discounts risk tax errors ‚Äî use only as last resort.

STEP 5: Confirm & Complete Sale
ACTION: Verify discount/tax on POS/invoice.
DETAIL:
‚Ä¢ Confirm reduced total & tax with customer.
‚Ä¢ Proceed to payment/receipt.
SYSTEM: POS ‚Üí Print receipt (shows discount)
‚ö† CRITICAL: If tax looks wrong ‚Üí void & re-test with pre-configured method.
